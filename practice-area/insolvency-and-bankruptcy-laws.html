Insolvency and Bankruptcy Laws

Our firm specializes in navigating the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), 2016, with a highly experienced team led by Mr. Saji P John. This dedicated unit includes a Qualified Insolvency Resolution Professional (IRP) and over ten seasoned lawyers who handle Corporate Insolvency Resolution Process (CIRP) and Liquidation compliance daily. We possess deep experience before the NCLT and NCLATs, guiding all key stakeholders such as Financial Creditors, Operational Creditors, Home Buyers and Corporate Applicant through the initiation of insolvency proceedings under Sections 7, 9, 10, 94, and 95. Our services extend to providing Legal Opinions and appearances for Resolution Professionals and Liquidators before the Courts. We are committed to efficient CIRP management including preparation and obtaining approval for Resolution plans in strict adherence to statutory deadlines. Our Firm has been expertly advising on Voluntary Liquidation (Section 59) which is yet another route for winding up of Companies under IBC. Our team's practical perspective is enhanced by members who have served as active liquidators and IRPs, providing a complete, start-to-finish understanding of the process.

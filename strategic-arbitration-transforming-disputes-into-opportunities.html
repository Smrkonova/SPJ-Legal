Strategic Arbitration: Transforming Disputes Into Opportunities

At SPJ Legal Advocates, we emphasis in transforming complex disputes into strategic opportunities through our Arbitration Advisory and Litigation services. Our seasoned professionals guide clients through the intricacies of arbitration, offering tailored solutions that align with their unique needs and objectives. From meticulously drafting enforceable arbitration clauses to representing clients in high-stakes proceedings, we ensure that every step is handled with precision and expertise. Our comprehensive approach encompasses pre-arbitration strategy, negotiation, and post-award enforcement, providing clients with a seamless experience that prioritizes efficiency and confidentiality. With a proven track record across various sectors, including commercial contracts, construction, joint ventures, and intellectual property, we are committed to delivering outcomes that protect and advance our clients' interests. In addition to our core services, SPJ Legal Advocates has a substantial portfolio of high-profile and contested arbitration proceedings. We also focus in the enforcement of foreign arbitral awards in India under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. Our team adeptly navigates the complexities of Part II of the Act, ensuring that foreign awards are recognized and enforced in Indian courts, subject to compliance with the stipulated conditions. This includes presenting duly authenticated copies of the award and the original Arbitration Agreement and addressing any grounds for refusal as outlined in Section 48 of the Act.
